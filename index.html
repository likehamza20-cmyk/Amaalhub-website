About Me

Amaal HUB is a growing clothing brand from Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu the heart of India’s textile industry. We specialize in cotton leggings and women’s wear, crafted with comfort, durability, and style in mind. By connecting directly with trusted manufacturers, we ensure high-quality products at affordable prices for both retail and wholesale customers.
